My Appliance is Smoking

Shut off the circuit breaker to the appliance. If smoking persists or the situation seems unmanageable, call your local fire department and exit the building. Once the situation is safe, call for service.

My Appliance Shocked Me

Shut off the circuit breaker, do not attempt to unplug the unit if it requires touching/laying over appliance. Call for service.

I Smell Gas

If you smell gas while you are using your appliance, shut the appliance off. If the smell persists or if no one was using the appliance and you smell gas, exit the building and call the gas company immediately. Call for service after situation is deemed safe.

My Appliance is Leaking/Flooding

If you are using the appliance, turn the appliance off. If the leaking persists, locate the water shutoff for the appliance and shut the water off. If the leak is unmanageable, call your local fire department. Call for service after water is off.
