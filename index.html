Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are bonded, licensed and insured.

What appliances do you repair?

We specialize in refrigerator repair, stove repair, oven repair, cooktop repair, microwave repair, dishwasher repair, washer repair, and dryer repair. 

How do you charge for the services?

We are proud to offer flat rate, upfront pricing options which would include labor and parts. Instead of charging by the hour, we charge you one flat rate for the job itself. In addition, we are upfront with how much a service will cost. Before we begin any work on your behalf, we will provide you with an accurate price quote.

How do I book a service?

To set up an appointment, for general inquiries, please feel free to contact us directly:

  1. Fill out our secure, online Contact Form
  2. By Phone: 718-200-0540
  3. By Email: support@nycappliancecare.com

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (e.g. Visa, Mastercard, American Express), personal and business checks, and cash.

What brands you do not service?

We service the most popular brands of appliances. However, we do not service Blomberg, Avanti, Summit appliances.
